Our Story

Where the Ocean Meets Your Table

Nestled right on the stunning white sands of Nacpan Beach in El Nido, Palawan, Fisherman's Restaurant is your ultimate beachside dining escape. Sink your toes in the sand while savoring freshly-caught seafood, tropical cocktails, and authentic Filipino favorites — all with an unobstructed view of the crystal-blue West Philippine Sea.

We also deliver within Nacpan and Calitang Beach, bringing the taste of paradise right to your doorstep. Bulk orders are warmly welcome.
